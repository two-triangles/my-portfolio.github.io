< Why Coding? >

I have spent the majority of my life being a professional musician and it has been a truly wonderful experience. I have been able to travel the world, meet people from so many cultures and create memories that I will never ever forget!

I have always loved anything to do with problem solving, whether it be crosswords, chess or a good old fashioned sudoku. Like many people, the global pandemic left me in a position where a career change was definitely needed.

Coding is something that I have dabbled with throughout the years and it felt like the most natural progression to move into this line of work. It allows me to stay creative as well as positioning me around people who are brilliant and inspire me every day.
